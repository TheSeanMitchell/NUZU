Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2026 • Last updated: April 2026
Contact: TheSeanMitchell@protonmail.com
The short version: NUZU News is a news aggregator. We do not host articles, sell your data, or require account registration. We store your preferences (dark mode, saved articles, collapsed sections) locally on your device only.

1. Who we are

NUZU News ("NUZU," "we," "us," or "our") is an independent news aggregation service available at theseanmitchell.github.io/NUZU/ and as a mobile application. NUZU aggregates and links to publicly available news articles from third-party publishers. We do not create original news content.

2. Information we collect

Information we do NOT collect:

  • We do not require account registration or collect your name, email, or password to use NUZU.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with advertisers or third parties for marketing purposes.
  • We do not track your reading history on our servers.

Information stored locally on your device (not transmitted to us):

  • Display preferences (dark/light mode setting)
  • Saved/bookmarked article URLs and titles
  • Collapsed section preferences
  • Scroll position (for resuming your place)
  • Recently read article links (for visual dimming)

All of the above is stored using your browser's localStorage and never transmitted to NUZU or any third party. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser/app data.

3. Third-party content and links

NUZU links to articles hosted by third-party publishers (Reuters, AP, BBC, NYT, etc.). When you click a link and visit a publisher's website or app, that publisher's own privacy policy governs the data they collect. NUZU is not responsible for third-party privacy practices.

NUZU embeds YouTube live streams in its video feed. YouTube is operated by Google LLC and subject to Google's Privacy Policy. YouTube may set cookies or collect viewing data when video embeds are loaded.

4. Hosting and infrastructure

NUZU is hosted on GitHub Pages (operated by GitHub, Inc., a subsidiary of Microsoft). GitHub may collect standard server logs including IP addresses and browser user agents as part of normal web hosting operations. See GitHub's Privacy Statement for details.

5. News feed data

NUZU fetches publicly available RSS feeds from news publishers to display headlines. No personal data is transmitted during this process. RSS feed fetching is performed server-side (during site build/update) and the results are served as static files.

6. Push notifications (optional)

If you choose to enable push notifications, NUZU will request your permission through your browser or operating system. A notification subscription token is generated by your device and may be stored to deliver breaking news alerts. You can disable push notifications at any time through your device or browser settings. We do not use notification data for advertising or share it with third parties.

7. Newsletter (optional)

If you subscribe to the NUZU newsletter, your email address is collected via a third-party form service and used solely to send news digests. We do not share your email with advertisers. You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in any newsletter email.

8. Analytics (if enabled)

NUZU may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand aggregate usage (e.g., which sections are most popular, page load performance). Any analytics used will be disclosed here. We do not use analytics to build individual user profiles or for advertising targeting.

9. Children's privacy

NUZU is a general news service intended for audiences 13 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted personal information to us, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. California residents (CCPA)

California residents have the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete personal information, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. NUZU does not sell personal information. Because we collect minimal personal data (preferences stored locally on your device), most CCPA rights are satisfied by clearing your browser data. For any questions, contact us at TheSeanMitchell@protonmail.com.

11. European users (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, or Switzerland: NUZU's lawful basis for any data processing is legitimate interest (providing a news aggregation service) and/or consent (where you have explicitly provided information such as a newsletter email). You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, or object to processing of your personal data. Contact us at TheSeanMitchell@protonmail.com to exercise these rights.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy on this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of NUZU after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
